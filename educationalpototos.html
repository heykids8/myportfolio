Immigration has been a big part in the United States.

discrimination across the United States.Schools were affected by this too.

when the Great Migration was going on, and more and more
African Americans were moving up North, many did not like this,
causing many feuds and riots, especially in Chicago,
where riots left 38 people dead and over 50 wounded and/or injured.
Again, this affected schools, causing discrimination and segregation.
Busing was also created somewhere in the 1950's. This was the practice
of assigning African Americans to schools in the
suburbs in an effort to stop racial segregation.
Unfortunately, this didn't work.
